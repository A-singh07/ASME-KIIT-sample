JOIN US

Team Ketav, an illustrious society of KIIT university, school of mechanical engineering is the only society in KIIT university which is solely focused on the manufacturing and development of human powered vehicles. Team Ketav is affiliated to the prestigious American Society of mechanical engineers(ASME). Since it's inception in (2014), team Ketav has been one of the most consistently performing teams of the university. The team has won laurels in numerous tech fests and competitions, and sought recognition across the nation for it's exemplary work ethic, innovative designs and a steely mindset to promote the concept of human powered vehicles and their importance across the society. Following the motto: "passion. Ambition. Determination. "; this team is well and truly dedicated towards the goal of gaining sound expertise and excellence in their feild.

The biggest takeaways that students achieve from being a part of this team are learning about the various techniques and procedures required in designing such vehicles. Students also get accomplished in the areas of presentation, report writing and product pitching, amongst other soft skills, all of which are extremely for an individual tocbe successful in the industrial scenario today.

Who we are

"If you're still in control, you're still not going fast enough."
~ Mario Andretti.

The team ASME KIIT introduces itself, with the objective of achieving the zone that Andretti thought wasn't possible, top speed with total control.
ASME-KIIT is a student run organization that focuses on the manufacturing and development of human powered recumbent vehicles. The society is affiliated to the prestigious American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME). The team participates in the annual Asia Pacific Human Powered Vehicle Challenge E fest (HPVC E-Fest) and other similar events, and has been a winner in several editions.
